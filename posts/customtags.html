Pins control signals, not power. Always add a current-limiting resistor to LEDs. Do NOT power motors directly from microcontroller pins. Use color-coded wires to debug circuits faster. Why does PWM dim an LED even though voltage stays 5V? Because the duty cycle reduces effective power. Simple to prototype on breadboard. Not stable for RF or high-speed signals.
