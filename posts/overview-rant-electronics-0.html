Overview [Rant Electronics #0]

“Engineering tantrums dressed as insights.”

May 16, 2025

You might be guessing why this student created a weirdly-titled series like this?
Well, keep guessing. I ain't telling you.

Yep—guess.

What, you thought I’d just spill it in the second line like some obedient tech blogger?
Nah. Sit tight, I’ll tell you in line ten. Or maybe fifteen. Who knows? I lost count.

First, you need to know: my brain is officially fried. I’ve entered the stage of academic madness where nothing makes sense and everything pisses me off.

See, this isn’t your clean, organized, “5 Things You Must Know About Resistors” kinda series.
This is a digital vent chamber. A hot mess of curiosity, frustration, insomnia, and burnt PCBs.

Why do we even have to study this crap? What even is a transistor?
Why is a thing called an “amplifier” not available at juice shops?
Who named half these components anyway—were they high?

This series is just me ranting out loud, turning my engineering breakdowns into something maybe educational. Maybe not. I don’t really care.
If you learn something, that’s your fault. I clearly told you in the twelevth line not to take this seriously. Didn’t you read?

If you’re expecting “value,” “clarity,” or “goals”—lmao.
This is me screaming into the void, because honestly, I have no one else. Also don’t mind me highlighting the most random stuff, thats just engineering.

Now that your lazy brain is warming up, here's what you're being dragged into:

  1. Why I’m mad – Not optional. You will listen, my love.

  2. The core idea – Basically, me showing off obscure stuff about basic things and flexing like I know sh*t.

  3. Real-world relevance – So you stop asking “oH wHeRe dO wE uSe tHiS?” while I boil in rage.

  4. History & philosophy – Because yes, when I stay up tinkering at 3 AM, voices do talk to me.

  5. Deep-dives into random things – Why is the switch plastic? What kind of plastic? Why that color? Who thought this shape was ergonomic?

  6. Pure chaos – Tangents. Side stories. Existential dread. Coils and caffeine.

And no, this isn’t some gimmick. This is real pain turned into chaotic storytelling.
Every article is a deep, sometimes messy, sometimes poetic, sometimes hilarious look at the everyday electronics we’ve taken for granted.
One component at a time.

So buckle up.
You’re entering a world where engineers scream into the void, and somehow, it echoes back in Ohm’s Law.

Welcome to Rant-Electronics.
This is not a tutorial.
This is therapy.
